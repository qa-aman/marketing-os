# Template - 12-slide marketing strategy deck

A reusable outline Cowork fills in with brand context and real data. Used in Lesson 2.

1. **Title** - brand, "Marketing Strategy", quarter and year
2. **Where we are** - current state in 3 numbers (audience, revenue mix, top channel)
3. **The market** - 3 trends shaping our category right now
4. **Our customer** - primary persona, one slide, plain language
5. **Positioning** - the one-liner and where we sit vs competitors
6. **Goals this quarter** - 2 to 3 measurable goals
7. **Growth priorities** - the 3 bets we are making
8. **Channel plan** - what we do on each channel, one row each
9. **Content themes** - 3 to 4 themes tied to the customer's awareness stages
10. **Campaign calendar** - the quarter at a glance
11. **Metrics** - what we measure and the target for each
12. **Next steps** - who does what by when

## Rules for Cowork

- Use the brand photos provided, do not invent imagery
- Pull positioning and customer detail from the context files, do not generalize
- Keep each slide to one idea, no walls of text
- Use the brand color as an accent only
