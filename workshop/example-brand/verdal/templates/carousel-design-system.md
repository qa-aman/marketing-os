# Template - carousel design system

Rules Cowork follows every time it builds an Instagram carousel for the brand. Used in Lesson 3.

## Structure (7 slides)

1. **Hook** - a question or surprising line that stops the scroll
2. **The problem** - name what the reader is seeing or feeling
3. **The why** - the one-sentence reason it happens
4-6. **The fix** - one clear step per slide, plain language
7. **Soft close** - the takeaway and a gentle next step

## Layout rules

- One idea per slide. If it needs two, it's two slides.
- Headline in sentence case, large, top third of the slide.
- Body text short. Never more than two lines.
- Brand green as the anchor color, terracotta for accents and highlights, cream background.
- Consistent margins on every slide. Nothing touching the edge.
- Readable on a phone at arm's length. If you have to squint, the text is too small.

## Voice

- Follow brand-voice.md exactly.
- Lead with the plant, not the product.
- No hype, no shame, no emoji walls.
