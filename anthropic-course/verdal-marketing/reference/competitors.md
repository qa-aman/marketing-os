# Competitor notes - Verdal

Reference for competitor research, digests, and positioning. Used in Session 5.

## The Sill
- Site: thesill.com
- What they sell: plants plus pots and accessories, and some plant care content.
- Audience: design-led urban plant owners, similar to ours.
- Strength: strong brand and photography, big Instagram following.
- Gap for us: their care advice is light. We can win on the "why" behind plant care.

## Bloomscape
- Site: bloomscape.com
- What they sell: plants delivered to your door, plus care guides.
- Audience: beginners who want plants shipped ready to go.
- Strength: delivery experience and a large care library.
- Gap for us: they are broad, not focused on nutrition and ongoing care like Verdal.

## Leon and George
- Site: leonandgeorge.com
- What they sell: premium plants and planters, design-forward.
- Audience: higher end, interior-design minded.
- Strength: premium positioning and visual identity.
- Gap for us: they are about the object, we are about keeping the plant thriving.

## How Verdal is different
We are not a plant shop. We are the brand that keeps the plants you already own healthy: vet-of-plants nutrition, tools, and a monthly Care Box, explained in plain language for people who treat their plants like pets.
