**From:** Maya (content)
**To:** Marketing team
**Subject:** Plant-care trends this month - a few things worth a post

Hi team,

Pulled the latest category numbers, three things stood out.

1. **Winter watering is the top worry.** Searches for "why are my leaves yellow" and "am I overwatering" are up around 40 percent this month. People are watering on their summer schedule and their plants are resting. Big chance for a simple "do less right now" post.

2. **Less-is-more advice is what people want.** The content that did best across the category was calm and reassuring, not "10 rules you are breaking". Our voice already fits this, we should lean in.

3. **Root health is getting attention.** A few larger accounts posted about roots and recovery after the cold snap. Our Root Recovery Tonic ties in naturally, without being salesy.

Might be worth a short newsletter section and a carousel. Happy to help.

Maya
