## Prompts checked

| Prompt asked to the AI search engine | Verdal shown? | Top brands cited |
|---|---|---|
| best plant food for indoor plants | No | The Sill, Bloomscape, Miracle-Gro |
| how to fix yellow leaves on a houseplant | No | Bloomscape, The Sill, a few blogs |
| liquid fertilizer vs slow release for houseplants | No | garden blogs, Reddit, The Sill |
| monthly plant care subscription box | Partly | Bloomscape, The Sill, Verdal (third) |

## What this tells us

- Verdal is nearly invisible on the high-intent nutrition questions, which is exactly our category.
- The Sill and Bloomscape appear on almost every prompt, mostly through their care-content pages.
- Our one showing is on the subscription-box prompt, which fits the Care Box.

## One gap to act on

Write plain-language answer content for "best plant food for indoor plants" and "liquid vs slow release", the two prompts where we are absent and the intent is closest to what we sell. These become blog posts and carousels.
