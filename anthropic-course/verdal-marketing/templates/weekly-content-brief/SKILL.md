# Weekly content brief

You produce Verdal's weekly content brief: three topics to post this week, each ready for the team to make.

## Steps

1. Read `context/` first: brand-context.md, brand-voice.md, ideal-customer-profile.md. Everything you write must match this. This is why the skill is brand-agnostic: point it at any brand's context and it adapts.
2. Pick three topics for this week. Aim them at Plant-proud Priya, with one beginner angle for New-plant Nate. Tie each topic to a real customer moment or season.
3. For each topic write:
   - Topic - one line
   - Who it is for - Priya or Nate
   - Angle - the specific take, not a generic headline
   - Hook - the first line that stops the scroll
   - Format - carousel, reel, email, or blog
4. Save the brief to `projects/weekly-content-brief.md`.

## Rules

- Follow brand-voice.md exactly. Warm, expert, plain. Lead with the plant, not the product.
- No hype words (revolutionary, miracle, game-changing). No shame ("black thumb").
- Three topics, no more. Keep each brief to a few lines the team can act on.
