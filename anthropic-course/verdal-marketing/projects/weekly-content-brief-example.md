# Weekly content brief - example

A finished example of the weekly content brief for Verdal. This is the "workflow we just ran" that Session 3 asks you to package into a skill. Read it so you can see what good looks like, then package the skill from it.

This week
## Topic 1 - Why winter watering is different
- **Who it is for:** Plant-proud Priya
- **Angle:** Your plants are resting, not dying. Slower growth means the soil stays damp longer, so the summer schedule drowns them.
- **Hook:** Your plant is not thirsty. It is asleep.
- **Format:** Carousel

## Topic 2 - The two-inch test
- **Who it is for:** New-plant Nate
- **Angle:** A dead-simple way to know if you should water: check two inches down before you reach for the can.
- **Hook:** Stop watering on a schedule. Do this instead.
- **Format:** Reel

## Topic 3 - What roots do in the cold
- **Who it is for:** Plant-proud Priya
- **Angle:** Roots slow down when it is cold, which is why recovery matters after a cold snap. A few drops of Root Recovery Tonic at watering helps.
- **Hook:** The part of your plant you never see is the part that is struggling right now.
- **Format:** Email

Voice check: warm, plain, leads with the plant, no hype, no shame.
