**Subject:** Your plant's quietest season is now

Hi there,

Winter light is low, and your plants know it. Most of them are resting, not dying, so this is the month to do less, not more.

## This month's one thing
Ease off the water. Shorter days mean slower growth, and slower growth means thirstier-looking soil that is actually still damp. Check two inches down before you reach for the watering can.
