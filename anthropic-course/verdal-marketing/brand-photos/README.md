# brand-photos

Real plant photos Cowork uses when it builds the strategy deck in Session 1 and visual content later. These are Creative Commons images, fine for the workshop and internal demos.

Photos included:
- monstera-hero.jpg - a bright indoor plant, good hero shot
- potted-plants.jpg - a wall of potted plants, good for range or background
- lifestyle-repotting.jpg - hands repotting a plant, good for how-to content
- lifestyle-watering.jpg - watering plants, warm and bright

For a client-facing result, replace these with your own product and lifestyle photos. Keep the file names simple and descriptive so Cowork can pick the right one, for example `care-box-product.jpg` or `priya-with-monstera.jpg`. Then delete the ones you do not need.
