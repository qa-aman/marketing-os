# Brand context - Verdal

## What we are

Verdal is a premium indoor plant care brand. We sell plant nutrients (liquid fertilizer, root tonic, bloom booster), precision tools (moisture meter, shears, watering can), and a monthly Care Box subscription. Direct-to-consumer through verdal.com, plus a small number of premium plant shops and garden centers.

Founded 2021. Team of 12. Based in Portland, Oregon. Ships across the US.

## What we sell

| Line | Products | Price range |
|---|---|---|
| Nutrients | All-Purpose Plant Food, Root Recovery Tonic, Bloom Booster | $16 - $24 |
| Tools | Moisture meter, precision shears, long-spout watering can | $19 - $42 |
| Care Box | Monthly subscription: seasonal nutrients + a care card | $29 / month |

Bestseller: All-Purpose Plant Food. Highest margin: the Care Box subscription.

## Positioning

For people who keep a lot of houseplants and treat them like pets. We sit between cheap generic plant food (grocery-aisle blue crystals) and intimidating horticulture supply shops. Premium but approachable. Every product is formulated with a horticulturist, and that is our proof, not our personality.

**One-liner:** Plant care for people who actually talk to their plants.

## Where we are now

- ~31,000 email subscribers
- ~26% of revenue from the Care Box subscription
- Instagram is the strongest channel; Pinterest and TikTok are growing
- Main goal this year: grow Care Box subscriptions and lower acquisition cost

## Competitors

- **The Sill** - plants plus accessories, strong brand, similar audience
- **Bloomscape** - plant delivery plus care content, broad range
- **Léon and George** - design-led, premium, lifestyle positioning

## Brand color

Deep botanical green (`#2F6B4F`) as primary. Cream and terracotta as supporting. Use green as the anchor and terracotta as a warm accent. Do not flood every element with green.
