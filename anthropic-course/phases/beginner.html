Meet the tool and make it yours. Two sessions. By the end you hand Cowork a real task and it already knows your brand.

Who this is for. Everyone starts here, no AI or technical experience needed. The only prerequisite is Session 0: Cowork installed and the Verdal folder on your laptop. If that is not done, do the setup guide first.

Session 1 - Meet Claude Cowork

The task loop · setup and permissions · files · the model picker

The mental shift: Chat hands you text to copy, Cowork does the work and saves the finished file into your folder. You watch its to-do list, steer it, and grant it access to one folder only.

  • Why Cowork is a different tool from the chat you already know
  • The task loop: it plans, works step by step, and saves
  • Watch the instructor build a 12-slide Verdal strategy deck live
  • Your first task on your own laptop
You can now: hand Cowork a task, steer it, and find the finished file in projects/.

Session 2 - Standing context & projects

Global instructions · Projects with memory · build your brand files

Stop repeating yourself. Set rules once that apply to every session, and create a Project that remembers your brand, context, and work. The standout move: point Cowork at an empty folder and let it interview you to write your brand files.

  • Global instructions: the rules Cowork follows every time
  • Projects: a reusable workspace with its own memory
  • Let Cowork build your brand-context, brand-voice, and ICP files by asking you questions
You can now: set standing rules and build a Project that keeps your brand on hand, without re-explaining it.
