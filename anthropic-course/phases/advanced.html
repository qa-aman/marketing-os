  • Scheduled tasks: a weekly job that runs on its own

Session 6 - Safety, sharing & next steps

Permission modes · sharing a skill · human-in-the-loop · two models together

Work safely, hand your best skill to a colleague, and see where this goes after the workshop.

  • Permission modes and reviewing output before it ships
