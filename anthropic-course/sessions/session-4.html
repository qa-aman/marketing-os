What connectors do

A connector gives Cowork live access to a tool, worked the same way as your files.

You click it on. You sign in once. Then you ask Cowork to use it, just like you ask it to read a local document.

A note on access

Grant the minimum access a tool asks for. Read-only is usually enough.

You can revoke any connector at any time in Customize > Connectors. Cowork asks before it does anything outside your folder.
When you connect Gmail
Allow Claude to read your Gmail messages and threads? Cowork will not send emails on your behalf.
Choose read-only where the option appears. Sending and editing permissions are optional.
