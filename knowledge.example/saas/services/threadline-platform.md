# Service: Threadline Platform

## What it is

A customer onboarding platform for product-led B2B SaaS. Configures in-product onboarding flows, milestone tracking, and contextual nudges without engineering work.

## Who it's for

Product-led B2B SaaS companies, post-Series A, 50-2000 employees, with a self-serve trial or freemium motion.

## Buyer

- **Primary**: Head of Growth or Head of Product
- **Influencer**: PMs, lifecycle marketers, growth engineers
- **Blocker**: VP Engineering (worried about another vendor in the stack)
- **Champion**: usually a growth-focused PM or PLG lead

## Core features

### 1. In-product checklists
Drag-and-drop builder for onboarding checklists. Tracks completion per user. Triggers next-step nudges.

### 2. Contextual tours
Tooltip and modal sequences tied to user state. Shows the right tour at the right moment, not on first login.

### 3. Milestone tracking
Define what "activated" means for your product (e.g. "imported 10 contacts AND invited 1 teammate"). Tracks each user against the definition. Reports cohort activation rates.

### 4. Multi-channel nudges
In-app notifications, email, and Slack DMs for stuck users. Configured by user state, not blast schedules.

### 5. Activation analytics
Funnel reports per onboarding flow. Drop-off analysis. Cohort comparison. Connects to the data warehouse.

## Common objections and responses

- **"Why not build this in-house?"** Most teams underestimate the cost. Building activation tracking, segmentation, and nudge orchestration is 4-6 engineer months. We've benchmarked.
- **"How is this different from Pendo or Appcues?"** Pendo is for analytics-led teams who want broad product insights. Appcues is older, more focused on tours and modals. We're built for PLG growth teams whose day-to-day metric is activation, not "feature adoption."
- **"We already have Intercom."** Intercom is great for support. We're complementary: Intercom handles inbound questions, Threadline drives proactive activation.
