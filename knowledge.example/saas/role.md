- Prefer experiments to debate. Run small, learn fast.
- Hate corporate language. Hate filler. Hate decks with 47 slides.
- Move fast: a campaign brief should take 30 minutes, not a week.

## What I want from this OS

- Compress the time from idea to shipped asset
