## What we do (in plain English)

B2B SaaS companies lose 40-60% of trial users in the first 7 days because onboarding is broken: too many empty states, no guided activation, no in-app coaching. Threadline gives PLG teams the building blocks to fix this without engineering: in-product checklists, contextual tours, milestone tracking, and Slack/email nudges, all configured by the PM or growth team.

## Who we sell to

Product-led B2B SaaS companies, post-Series A, with 50-2000 employees. Buyer is typically a Head of Growth, Head of Product, or PLG lead. Average deal size $32K ARR. Sales cycle 28 days.
