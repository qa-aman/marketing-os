## Q1 2026 launch campaign retro (15-04-2026)

**Result**: hit primary KPI. Generated 1,840 trial signups vs target 1,500 (+23%).

**Reusable insights**:
- Founder-voice LinkedIn posts pulled 4.1x engagement vs company-voice posts on the same topic. Applies to: any awareness content where authenticity matters.
- "How we built X" technical breakdowns outperformed "Here's why X matters" thought pieces 3x on engagement and 2x on signups. Applies to: when audience is technical (PMs, engineers).
- Webinar registration dropped 40% when title was "Introduction to..." vs "How to <achieve outcome> in <timeframe>." Applies to: all webinar titling.
- LinkedIn carousels with 6-8 slides outperformed 10-12 slides on saves. Applies to: all carousel content. Cap at 8 slides.

**Stop doing**:
- Generic "We're excited to announce" launch posts. Zero engagement compared to operator-narrative posts.
- 21-day email nurture sequences. Drop-off after email 3 was severe. Cap nurture at 5 emails over 14 days.
- Sending webinar reminders 7 days out. Attendance correlated more with day-of and day-before reminders.

**Test next time**:
- Founder-voice video clips on LinkedIn vs founder-voice text. Hypothesis: video bumps reach, text bumps quality.
- Different webinar lengths: 30 min vs 45 min vs 60 min. Hypothesis: 30 min wins on attendance, 45 wins on conversion.

## February newsletter relaunch retro (10-03-2026)

**Result**: partial. Open rate up 38% to 42% but subscriber growth slowed.

**Reusable insights**:
- Subject lines starting with a number outperformed without by 38% on open rate. Applies to: all email subject lines.
- Personal POV opener (first 80 words from the author) drove 3x more replies than industry-news opener. Applies to: all newsletters.
- Weekly cadence outperformed biweekly on engagement, but biweekly outperformed on subscriber growth (more shareability per issue). Applies to: cadence decisions.

**Stop doing**:
- News-recap-only issues. Subscribers said in survey they want POV, not aggregation.
- Round-robin author rotation. Voice consistency matters more than diverse perspectives.

**Test next time**:
- Mid-week (Wed) vs Tue send time. Current Tue at 9am ET.
- Adding a recurring "what I'm reading" segment vs not.

## Pendo competitive content campaign retro (28-02-2026)

**Result**: missed primary KPI. Goal was 200 demo bookings; hit 142.

**Reusable insights**:
- "Pendo vs Threadline" comparison page generated 8.4x more traffic than "Why we built Threadline" content. Applies to: comparison content is high-intent.
- Direct competitor naming in LinkedIn posts polarized audience: 2x engagement but 30% of comments were defensive. Net positive but watch the brand sentiment.

**Stop doing**:
- Long-form thought leadership about category problems. Audience preferred specific comparisons and tactical content during high-intent search.

**Test next time**:
- Comparison videos (5-min walk-throughs) vs comparison written content.
