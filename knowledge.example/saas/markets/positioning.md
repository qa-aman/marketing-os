# Positioning

## One-sentence positioning

For PLG B2B SaaS growth teams whose activation rate is stuck, Threadline is the customer onboarding platform that lets them ship experiments in days without engineering, unlike legacy adoption tools that were built for analytics, not activation.

## Components

- **Target customer**: PLG B2B SaaS growth teams (Maya persona)
- **Problem**: activation rate stuck, can't experiment without engineering
- **Category**: customer onboarding platform
- **Key benefit**: ship experiments in days without engineering
- **Alternative**: Pendo, Appcues, in-house build
- **Differentiator**: built for activation, not analytics; built for growth teams, not product ops

## What we are

The activation OS for PLG growth teams.

## What we are not

- Not a product analytics tool (that's Mixpanel, Amplitude, Heap)
- Not a help center (that's Intercom, Zendesk)
- Not a marketing automation platform (that's Customer.io, Braze, Iterable)
- Not a CDP (that's Segment, Rudderstack)
- Not a feature adoption tool (that's Pendo)
- Not a tour-builder (that's Appcues, UserPilot)

The distinction: feature adoption is about driving usage of any feature. Activation is about getting users to the moment they realize value. We do the second.

## Messaging hierarchy

### H1 (homepage hero)
"Turn trial signups into activated users in days"

### Sub
"Threadline gives PLG growth teams the building blocks to ship onboarding experiments without engineering, with cohort analytics built in."

### 3 supporting messages

1. **Ship in a week, not a quarter.** Most teams ship their first onboarding flow within 6 days of buying.
2. **Built for growth, not analytics.** Activation funnels, not feature heatmaps. Cohort comparison, not session replay.
3. **No engineering tax.** Configured by PMs and growth marketers. Engineering reviews the embed once.

## Proof points (cite these in content)

- 240+ B2B SaaS customers
- Customers lift day-7 activation 38% on average
- Average time to first shipped flow: 6 days
- 96% gross retention
- NPS 64

## Anti-positioning (where we don't compete)

- B2C apps: we don't pretend to be relevant
- Pre-Series A: ROI doesn't pencil at their scale
- Enterprise sales-led SaaS with no trial: not our motion
- Marketing automation use cases: we tell prospects to use Customer.io or Iterable, not us
