# Visual identity

## Colors

- **Primary**: #0F172A (deep navy, used for text and primary surfaces)
- **Accent**: #FF6B35 (orange, used for CTAs and highlights)
- **Neutral**: #F8FAFC (light gray, backgrounds)
- **Success**: #10B981
- **Warning**: #F59E0B
- **Error**: #EF4444

## Typography

- **Headings**: Inter, semibold, tight tracking
- **Body**: Inter, regular
- **Code/numbers**: JetBrains Mono
- **Never use**: serif fonts, script fonts, italics for emphasis (use bold instead)

## Logo rules

- Minimum size: 24px height for digital, 0.5 inch for print
- Clearspace: 1x logo height on all sides
- Never put on busy backgrounds. Solid color only.
- Wordmark only for favicons and small uses
- Never stretch, never rotate, never recolor outside the official palette

## Image style

- **Photography**: real product UI, real customers (with permission), real workspaces. No stock photos of "diverse team in glass conference room."
- **Illustrations**: minimal line art, single accent color, never cartoonish
- **Screenshots**: always show real data, anonymized. Never use Lorem Ipsum.
- **Charts**: 2D only, no 3D. Annotated with the takeaway, not just the data.

## Templates

Locations of brand templates:
- Slide deck: `templates/threadline-deck-2026.pptx`
- Email header: `templates/email-header.png`
- LinkedIn quote graphic: `templates/linkedin-quote.fig`
- Social cover: `templates/social-cover.png`

## What to avoid

- Stock photos of handshakes, lightbulbs, gears, rockets
- 3D illustrations
- Gradients (we are flat-color brand)
- Excessive iconography ("an icon for every feature")
- Drop shadows on text
