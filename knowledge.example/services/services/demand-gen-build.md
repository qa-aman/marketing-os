# Service: Demand Gen Build

## What it is

A 6-12 month embedded engagement that builds a B2B demand generation engine from the ground up. We sit inside the client's team and produce the system - ICP definition, messaging, content program, channel mix, and reporting - then hand it off running.

## Who it's for

- B2B SaaS companies, Series B or C, $15M-$80M ARR
- 1-3 person marketing team (or no marketing team at all)
- Pipeline currently dependent on founder network or one outbound channel
- Growth target that requires a predictable inbound motion within 12 months

## Who it's NOT for

- Pre-Series A companies (ROI doesn't pencil at that budget)
- Companies with 8+ person marketing teams (they have the capability - they need a different kind of help)
- Companies looking for a quick campaign, not a system
- B2C, consumer, or marketplace businesses

## Buyer

- **Primary**: VP of Marketing or Head of Marketing
- **Decision-maker**: CEO (signs the contract)
- **Influencer**: CRO if pipeline targets are shared

## What's included

### Phase 1: Foundation (weeks 1-6)
- ICP audit and definition (who we are actually selling to vs who we think we are)
- Messaging workshop (positioning, value props, proof points)
- Competitive landscape map
- Channel prioritization (where this ICP actually spends time)
- 90-day execution roadmap with measurable milestones

### Phase 2: Build (months 2-6)
- Content program launch: pillar strategy, editorial calendar, production
- SEO foundation: keyword map, content briefs, on-page optimization
- Email nurture: sequences for inbound leads, trial users, or event attendees
- LinkedIn organic program: company page + executive voice
- Reporting dashboard: pipeline attribution, content metrics, channel performance

### Phase 3: Scale and hand-off (months 7-12)
- Paid channel layer (LinkedIn ads, Google) once organic is producing signal
- Optimization: doubling down on what's working, cutting what isn't
- Playbook documentation: everything the internal team needs to run the system
- Hiring support: job descriptions, interview briefs, onboarding plan for the incoming hire

## Common objections and responses

- **"We tried an agency before and it didn't work."**
  Most agencies execute into a broken system. If your ICP, messaging, and positioning aren't right, no amount of channel execution fixes it. We fix the system first. That's why we start with a foundation phase, not a campaign.

- **"I'm not sure we're ready for this investment."**
  The question isn't whether you can afford $25K/month. It's whether you can afford 12 more months of founder-dependent pipeline. We've done the math with 40 clients. The cost of not building the system is almost always higher.

- **"Can we start with just the strategy?"**
  Yes. The GTM Strategy Sprint ($45K, 6 weeks) gives you the full foundation: ICP definition, positioning, channel prioritization, and 90-day roadmap. Many clients use it to de-risk the larger engagement decision.

- **"How is this different from hiring a VP of Marketing?"**
  A VP hire takes 3-6 months to find, 6-12 months to ramp, costs $200K+ in salary and equity, and is very hard to unwind if it's wrong. We start delivering in week one and are explicitly designed to de-risk the eventual hire by building the playbook first.

## What success looks like

End of engagement: the client has an ICP definition the whole company aligns on, a content program producing inbound pipeline, a channel mix with clear ROI visibility, and an internal team that knows how to run the system. Clearpath is optional from that point forward.
