# Visual identity

## Colors

- **Primary**: #1A1A2E (deep navy, text and primary surfaces)
- **Accent**: #E8C547 (warm gold, CTAs and highlights)
- **Neutral light**: #F5F4F0 (warm off-white, backgrounds)
- **Neutral mid**: #D4D0C8 (borders and dividers)
- **Success**: #2D6A4F
- **Alert**: #C77B3A

## Typography

- **Headings**: Freight Display Pro, regular weight, loose tracking
- **Body**: Inter, regular, 16px/1.6
- **Captions and labels**: Inter, medium, 13px
- **Never use**: decorative or script fonts, all-caps body copy

## Logo rules

- Minimum size: 120px width digital, 1.5 inch print
- Clearspace: 1x logo height on all sides
- Use on white, off-white, or deep navy only. Never on busy backgrounds.
- Wordmark and mark together for primary usage; mark alone for favicons and small formats
- Never recolor, stretch, or add effects

## Image style

- **Photography**: real workspaces, real whiteboards, real teams in candid moments. No posed stock photos.
- **Illustrations**: minimal, single-color line art on off-white. Never cartoonish.
- **Data visualization**: 2D only, annotated with the takeaway ("Pipeline grew 3.4x" on the chart, not just the axis label). Use brand colors only.
- **Screenshots**: real dashboards and docs, data anonymized where needed. Never Lorem Ipsum.
- **Diagrams**: clean boxes and arrows, navy and gold, white space generous. No 3D or drop shadows.

## Templates

- Proposal deck: `templates/clearpath-proposal-2026.pptx`
- Case study one-pager: `templates/case-study-template.pptx`
- LinkedIn quote graphic: `templates/linkedin-quote.fig`
- Email header: `templates/email-header.png`

## What to avoid

- Stock photos of handshakes, boardrooms, lightbulbs
- Busy backgrounds behind text
- More than 2 colors in any single chart
- Gradient fills
- Icons for everything (use words)
