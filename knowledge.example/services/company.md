## What we do (in plain English)

B2B companies at Series B and beyond hit a wall: the founder can't carry the pipeline anymore, the first marketing hire is overwhelmed, and nobody knows how to build a demand gen machine from scratch. Clearpath Advisory comes in for 6-12 months as an embedded team - part fractional CMO, part execution engine - and builds the system: ICP definition, messaging, content program, channel mix, and the reporting to know what's working. We leave behind a team that can run it without us.

## Who we hire for

Mid-market B2B SaaS companies, $15M-$150M ARR, Series B or C, who have:
- 1-3 person marketing team or no dedicated marketing function
- CEO/CRO who has been the de-facto CMO
- Pipeline that is too dependent on one channel or outbound only
- A growth target that requires a predictable inbound motion

Average engagement: $25K/month retainer, 9-month duration. Total contract value: $225K.

## Services

1. **GTM Strategy Sprint** (6 weeks, $45K fixed) - ICP audit, positioning workshop, channel prioritization, 90-day roadmap
2. **Demand Gen Build** (6-12 months, $20K-$35K/month) - embedded execution: content, SEO, paid, email, reporting
3. **Fractional CMO** (ongoing, $15K-$25K/month) - senior strategic leadership without a full-time hire
4. **Content Engine** (3-6 months, $15K/month) - standalone content program: strategy, production, distribution
