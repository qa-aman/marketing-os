# Role

**Role**: Director of Marketing
**Reports to**: CEO
**Team size**: 2 (1 content manager, 1 growth analyst)
**Tenure**: 8 months
**Hired to**: build Clearpath's own marketing function - the cobbler's children problem. The firm builds demand gen for clients but has relied entirely on referrals and the CEO's network for its own pipeline.

## What I'm measured on (top 3 KPIs)

1. Qualified pipeline generated (target: $1.2M in qualified opportunities per quarter by Q4 2026)
2. Content-attributed inbound leads (target: 40% of new business from inbound by Q3 2026, currently 12%)
3. Client retention and expansion (not my primary role, but content and thought leadership directly supports it)

## How I work

- Practitioner-first mindset. I write what I would want to read, not what sounds impressive in a deck.
- Allergic to vanity metrics. Impressions and followers do not move the needle for a consulting firm.
- Prefer doing over planning. A published piece beats a perfect brief every time.
- Direct feedback welcome. If a draft is weak, say so with specifics.
- Time is scarce. I have a team of 2. Every asset we produce needs to earn its production cost.

## What I want from this OS

- Help me produce thought leadership faster without sounding like an AI wrote it
- Give me a consistent content calendar I can actually stick to with a small team
- Help me repurpose the expertise locked in our consultants' heads into publishable content
- Track what's working so we stop repeating mistakes
