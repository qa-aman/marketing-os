# Positioning

## One-sentence positioning

For Series B and C B2B SaaS companies whose pipeline is still dependent on founder relationships, Clearpath Advisory is the embedded GTM consultancy that builds a repeatable demand generation engine in 9 months, unlike agencies that deliver reports or hires that take 12 months to ramp.

## Components

- **Target customer**: Series B/C B2B SaaS marketing leaders and CEOs
- **Problem**: pipeline that doesn't scale beyond founder network and one channel
- **Category**: embedded GTM consultancy
- **Key benefit**: a working demand gen system, not a strategy deck
- **Alternative**: in-house CMO hire, performance agency, generalist marketing consultant
- **Differentiator**: embedded model (we sit inside the team, not outside it), results in 90 days, we leave behind something that runs without us

## What we are

The firm you call when you need to build the marketing function, not optimize one.

## What we are not

- Not a creative agency (no brand work)
- Not a performance marketing shop (no paid-only engagements)
- Not a PR firm (no media relations)
- Not a fractional CMO firm only (we execute, not just advise)
- Not right for pre-Series A (ROI doesn't pencil below $10M ARR)
- Not right for companies with mature 8+ person marketing teams (they don't need us)

## Messaging hierarchy

### H1 (website hero)
"Build the marketing engine your sales team is waiting for."

### Sub
"Clearpath Advisory embeds with B2B SaaS teams at Series B and C to build demand gen systems that generate pipeline within 90 days - and keep running after we leave."

### 3 supporting messages

1. **Results in 90 days, not 9 months.** Most clients see their first content-attributed pipeline within the first quarter. We track this from day one.
2. **Embedded, not advisory.** We sit inside your Slack, your standups, your dashboards. We execute alongside your team, not from a separate office with a monthly readout.
3. **We leave behind a machine, not a dependency.** At the end of every engagement, your team can run the system without us. That's how we define success.

## Proof points (cite these in content)

- 40+ B2B SaaS clients across fintech, HR tech, dev tools, compliance, and operations
- Median time to first content-attributed opportunity: 67 days
- Average pipeline generated in first 6 months: $1.4M
- 82% of clients extend or expand after initial engagement
- 3 of 4 clients reduce agency spend by 40%+ after Clearpath engagement (consolidation effect)
- NPS: 71

## Anti-positioning (where we don't compete)

- We don't pitch against performance agencies on paid media ROI - different motion
- We don't compete on price with freelancers - different scope and accountability
- We don't go after enterprise CMO searches - different need, different buying process
